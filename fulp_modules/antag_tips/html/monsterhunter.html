You are a Monster Hunter!

Awoken from your retirement due to the presence of unholy creatures, you have a handful of abilities to deal with them.

You need a coffin to gain abilities, level up and protect yourself from Sol. In extreme cases, a locker or crate will serve for the latter.

Night has a duration of 10 minutes, and Sol strikes after that duration for 90 seconds.

Sol will burn you if you aren't in a coffin while it's ocurring, possibly to lethal levels if you're an elder bloodsucker.

As a bloodsucker, you can make crew members addicted to your blood to serve you using a persuasion rack, becoming your vassals. You can create a persuasion rack in the crafting menu after claiming a coffin.

Be cautious, as there might be monster hunters onboard seeking to end your existence.

Don't be afraid to ask mentors for help using the mentorhelp button.
